Chess on a Klein bottle

Horizontal glide-reflection between ranks

The version of the Klein bottle which is closest to the standard chess board is obtained by connecting every square of the uppermost and lowermost rank to the square obtained by shifting horizontally with half of the board width. This corresponds to a glide-reflection below rank 1 and above rank 8 with a distance of 4 squares. In addition, the board has a vertical translation by 16 squares.

Horizontal glide-reflection within ranks

Another Klein bottle is obtained by a glide-reflection within ranks 1 and 8 by 8 squares. This doubles the ranks 2 to 7 and gives a vertical translation by 14 squares.
